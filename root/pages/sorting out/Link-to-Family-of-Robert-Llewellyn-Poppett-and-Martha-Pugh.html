Family of Robert Llewellyn Poppett and Martha Pugh

Robert Llewellyn Poppett (RLP) was born 02 Apr 18061 in Worthen Shropshire, England, to Robert Poppit (1774-1838), a widower, and Elizabeth Llewellyn (1774-1836), both of Churchstoke. RLP was baptized 09 Apr 1806 in Churchstoke, Montgomeryshire, Wales.

RLP married Martha Pugh (b 1810 in Hope, Shropshire, England) on 10 Jun 1833 at All Saints Church, Worthen, Shropshire, England.2 He made his mark and Martha signed. Martha was a daughter of John Pugh (1761-1841) and Catherine Davies (1765-1844). 3

Their son John was born in 1834 in Shropshire. 4 Daughter Elizabeth was born in 1835 in Shrewsbury, Shropshire, England5.

Robert and Martha’s third child and second son, Robert Albert6 (RAP), was born 12 Apr 1839 in Bentlawnt, Shropshire, England7 and like his siblings, baptized at the All Saints’ Church in Worthen.

The family lived in Worthen, Shropshire in 1841. All 3 children were present in the household. Robert was a lead miner.8

Daughter Ann was born 18 Mar 1842 in Bentlawnt9.

Daughter Jane was born in Bentlawnt in 1843.10

Wife Martha died 25 Mar 184411; baby Jane died 5 Jun 184412, both in Bentlawnt. Per Martha’s death certificate, Robert was a shoemaker at this time.13

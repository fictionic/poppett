John Evans

Young Robert’s name can be found on the ship’s passenger list next to a Thomas Davis (Davies?), age 33, and near a John Evans who had a wife named Harriett, each of which is a likely candidate. We will recall that the priest who baptized RLP the first time in Shropshire in 1847 and then again in 1848 was a John Evans. Many John Evans’ eventually made the trek to Utah from Council Bluffs, but none that I can find with a Harriett. Perhaps it was she who died?
